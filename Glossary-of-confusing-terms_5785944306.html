About : Glossary of confusing terms

Created by Andrew Constable, last modified by Administrator on Apr 07, 2014

This page aims to standardise terminology used in SOKI and contains definitions to clarify terms and concepts that may have more than one meaning or for which a meaning may have different terms in different disciplines.  The source of confusion in terminology is described for each term where possible.  (when adding a term or concept - the term itself should be in style Heading 1, the following text can be linked to another site if preferable.  Alternative terms should be included if this is the case.  Alternative meanings can also be included and referenced with other terms.)
